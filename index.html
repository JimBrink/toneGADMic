Tone Generator and Display (version 2.1.4)

A tool that can generate sounds and display their wave form and harmonics. The help file contains a tour of the controls, discussion of the importance of harmonics and their use, and some experiments.

This version allows you to input sounds with your computer microphone. Their wave form will be shown along with their harmonics.

Special note: To input sounds, you need a microphone which may be built into a laptop but connected separately if you have a desktop. But even if you don't have one it will still generate sounds and show their wave forms and harmonics.

If you don't want to use a microphone, you may prefer to use regular version of toneGAD and its help file.
